About KSST

🪷 Shri Matre Namah! Om Sri Gurubhyo Namaha 🪷

Kovai Swamināma Smarana Trust (KSST) is dedicated to celebrating and sharing the timeless wisdom of Sanataṇa Dharma through structured classes, satsangs, and spiritual events that nurture devotion, discipline, and inner growth. All our classes are offered free of cost, creating a supportive space for seekers to learn and progress on their spiritual journey.

Our sessions are conducted with the blessings and guidance of Pujya Swamini Atmaprakashananda Saraswati, whose teachings illuminate the depth of Vedic scriptures, Sahasranāmas, and sacred chants. Through her grace, seekers are inspired to deepen their understanding and live a life rooted in devotion, clarity, and dharma.
